About BioZone
BioZone is an application that you can use to better the world by participating in conservation projects in your area. Within the app, you can see all the projects happening near you. When you help out at a project, you can gain points (and the satisfaction of keeping your earth clean). Helping with conservation projects in your area, no matter how small, can help the enviornment and assist in slowing down global warming,
